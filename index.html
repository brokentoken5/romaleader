Augustus and his life

Augustus was fought beside his great uncle Julius Cesar. Julius Cesar was assassinated. Augustus immediately took rule after Julius's death.

Important events, and enemies

Augustus had 2 main enemies Antony and Cleopatra. They wanted the throne after Julius died.
Thats why Augustus established the Second Triumvirate. despite the enemies he also did good things, like building roads, conquered more land and expanded rome, and improved the overall roman army
